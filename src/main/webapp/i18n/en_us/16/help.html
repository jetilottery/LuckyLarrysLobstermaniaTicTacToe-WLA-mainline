Lucky Larry's Lobstermania Tic Tac Toe

Trawl the seas for treasure in Lucky Larry’s Lobstermania Tic Tac Toe! Press the Lobster to reveal symbols, a Lucky Larry portrait, or treasure chests. Each Lucky Larry portrait reveals four identical symbols, and each treasure chest reveals two different symbols in a single symbol position. Reveal three identical symbols in any row, column or diagonal line to win!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Lucky Larry’s Lobstermania Tic Tac Toe is to find three identical symbols in a horizontal, vertical or diagonal line.

Press the Lobster to reveal symbols, a Lucky Larry portrait, or treasure chest symbols. Press the Lucky Larry portrait to reveal four identical symbols. Press each treasure chest to reveal two different symbols in a single symbol position – both symbols are used to complete winning lines, where applicable.

Reveal three identical symbols in any row, column, or diagonal line to win the corresponding prize.

Play ends when all portraits have been revealed. A message appears indicating the game’s outcome.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Press STOP to continue the game in manual play mode.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
